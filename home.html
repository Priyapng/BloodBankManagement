Blood Group Compatibility

Blood compatibility is important to ensure safe blood transfusion. Each blood group can donate or receive blood from specific groups only.

O- (Universal Donor)

Can donate to all blood groups. Mostly used in emergency cases.

O+

Can donate to O+, A+, B+, AB+.

A+

Can donate to A+ and AB+.

B+

Can donate to B+ and AB+.

AB+ (Universal Receiver)

Can receive blood from all groups.

AB-

Can donate to AB+ and AB-.
